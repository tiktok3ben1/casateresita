## Stained Glass Window

Inspired by the view from the back of the house, it represents Illimani, the emblematic mountain of La Paz, and the Libertad Bridge (one of the so-called ‘Trillizos’ bridges), which connects the San Jorge neighborhood (Av. Arce) with Miraflores (Av. Los Leones).
Design: Ernesto Gamarra F.
Execution: Manuel Segales, stained-glass artist.
