On this exhibition we can see the following pictures:
- Teresita at 9 Years old
- Teresita with her classmates of Colegio Aleman Mariscal Braun at their graduation (1950)
- Rosa de las Muñecas (Teresita's mother) on her trip to Niagara waterfalls with Patiño Family.
- Don José María Gamarra (Juan's Father) Yungueño landowner
- Bertha Antezana (Juan's mother)
- Augusto Fajardo and Rosa in their wedding (Teresita's father and mother)
- A picture of the Patiño family, dedicated to Laura (Teresita's grandmother

Tere remembered with joy, the first time she went to the cinema in Oruro, where she lived during part of her childhood, to see The Wizard of Oz, the color movie.
