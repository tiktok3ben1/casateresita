Oil Painting by the artist Arturo Borda (1951) belonging to the family collection.

Arturo Borda was one of the most famous painters in Bolivia in the first half of the twentieth century, he was well-known by his fascination with Illimani mountain, he painted several versions of this mountain.

He was most at all known by his landscape's paintings, but after the Second World War he started painting scenes of combat military, and this painting on display is one of them, is a unique and exclusive piece of art, here we can appreciate the ability and technical details from a first class artist.
